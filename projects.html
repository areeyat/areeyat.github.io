  • analyzing crop diversity & food access

    This project was done as part of a collaboration with the UC Berkeley High School Ethnic Studies Initiative. My team and I used agricultural and food access data to analyze the relationship between Indigenous communities and food security in the United States. The goal for this project was to educate high schoolers on where their food comes from while providing an introductory lesson on data tools.
